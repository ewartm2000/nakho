Our Services

At Nakho Group, we offer a comprehensive range of accounting and taxation services to meet the diverse needs of our clients.

Personal Income Tax

Our experienced tax professionals provide expert advice and assistance with personal income tax planning and filing. We ensure compliance with South African tax laws and help individuals optimize their tax positions.

Corporate Tax Planning

We offer strategic corporate tax planning services to businesses of all sizes. Our team analyzes your financial situation to minimize tax liabilities, maximize deductions, and optimize overall tax efficiency. We help you stay compliant with tax regulations while maximizing your financial performance.

Bookkeeping and Financial Reporting

Our bookkeeping services help businesses maintain accurate financial records and comply with reporting requirements. We offer comprehensive financial reporting services, including balance sheets, income statements, and cash flow statements, to provide insights into your company's financial health and performance.

Payroll Management

We provide efficient payroll management solutions to streamline your payroll processes and ensure timely and accurate payment of salaries and wages. Our payroll experts handle all aspects of payroll administration, including calculating employee salaries, deductions, and taxes, to save you time and resources.

Auditing and Assurance Services

Our auditing and assurance services help businesses maintain transparency and accountability in their financial reporting. We conduct thorough audits to assess the accuracy and reliability of financial information, identify areas for improvement, and provide recommendations to enhance internal controls and mitigate risks.

Business Advisory

Our business advisory services provide strategic guidance and support to help businesses achieve their goals and overcome challenges. Whether you're planning for growth, restructuring operations, or navigating regulatory changes, our team offers personalized solutions to drive success and long-term sustainability.

Company Registration and Compliance

We assist entrepreneurs and businesses with company registration and compliance to ensure legal and regulatory compliance. From registering your company with the relevant authorities to obtaining necessary licenses and permits, we help you navigate the complexities of starting and operating a business in South Africa.
