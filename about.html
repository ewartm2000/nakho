About Nakho Group

Nakho Group is a leading provider of accounting, taxation, and auditing services in Johannesburg, South Africa. With a team of experienced professionals, we strive to deliver high-quality financial solutions to our clients.

Our mission is to assist businesses and individuals in navigating the complex landscape of finance and taxation, ensuring compliance with regulations and maximizing financial efficiency.

Founded in 2005 by Nhlanhla Nakhokonke Dube, a seasoned tax expert and member of the South African Institute of Taxation (SAIT), Nakho Group has established a reputation for excellence and reliability in the financial services industry.
