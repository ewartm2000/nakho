About Us

Nakho Group is a premier accounting and taxation firm based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We specialize in providing personalized financial solutions to businesses and individuals.

Our team of experienced professionals is committed to delivering high-quality services tailored to meet our clients' specific needs.

Our Services

Accounting Services

Our accounting services include bookkeeping, financial reporting, budgeting, and payroll processing. We ensure accurate and timely financial records to help businesses make informed decisions.

Taxation Services

We offer comprehensive taxation services, including tax planning, preparation of tax returns, and assistance with tax audits. Our goal is to minimize tax liabilities while ensuring compliance with tax laws and regulations.

Consulting Services

Our consulting services cover a wide range of financial matters, including business advisory, financial analysis, and strategic planning. We work closely with clients to identify opportunities for growth and improvement.
