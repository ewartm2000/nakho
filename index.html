About Nakho Group

Nakho Group is a leading provider of accounting, taxation, and auditing services in Johannesburg, South Africa. With a team of experienced professionals, we strive to deliver high-quality financial solutions to our clients.

Our mission is to assist businesses and individuals in navigating the complex landscape of finance and taxation, ensuring compliance with regulations and maximizing financial efficiency.

Our Services

At Nakho Group, we offer a comprehensive range of services tailored to meet the diverse needs of our clients:

  • Accounting: Our accounting services include bookkeeping, financial reporting, budgeting, and payroll management.
  • Taxation: We provide expert tax planning, preparation, and compliance services to minimize tax liabilities and ensure regulatory compliance.
  • Auditing: Our auditing services encompass financial statement audits, internal audits, and compliance reviews to provide assurance on financial integrity and regulatory compliance.
  • Financial Consulting: We offer strategic financial consulting services to help businesses optimize performance, streamline operations, and achieve their financial objectives.
  • Business Advisory: Our business advisory services cover a wide range of areas including strategic planning, risk management, and business process optimization to drive growth and profitability.
